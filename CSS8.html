Physics Wallah (PW), India’s leading ed-tech platform, is proud to announce a significant milestone for its skilling vertical, PW Skills. It has now emerged as the largest online platform for teaching tech skills to an impressive 1.5 lakh learners, offering courses in Hindi, English, and Hinglish. Among these learners, 50,000 are enrolled in paid batches, while the remaining have benefited from the free courses. Notably, 30,000 learners hail from the Hindi heartland, encompassing regions such as Uttar Pradesh, Bihar, Madhya Pradesh, and other states. Achieving this milestone within a mere five-month span is a testament to the power of PW’s existing community and digital distribution, which have been instrumental in driving this growth.
